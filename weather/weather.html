Weather in the city

Overview

London experiences a temperate maritime climate, characterized by mild winters and cool summers. Rainfall is evenly distributed throughout the year, so it’s always a good idea to carry an umbrella!

Traveler Tips

  • Carry a lightweight, waterproof jacket year-round.
  • Pack layers during spring and autumn to adjust to fluctuating temperatures.
  • In winter, bring a warm coat, gloves, and scarves.
  • Comfortable shoes are essential for exploring the city.

Seasonal Weather

Spring (March - May)

Spring is mild and pleasant, with average temperatures ranging from 8°C to 15°C. Perfect for exploring parks like Hyde Park and Kew Gardens.

Summer (June - August)

Summer is warm, with temperatures between 15°C and 25°C. Enjoy outdoor events, festivals, and Thames river cruises.

Autumn (September - November)

Autumn is cool and crisp, with vibrant foliage in parks. Temperatures range from 10°C to 18°C.

Winter (December - February)

Winter is chilly but rarely freezing, with temperatures between 2°C and 8°C. Perfect for festive markets and cozy pubs.
