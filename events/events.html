Major Annual Events

London is a city that never stops celebrating, offering a diverse calendar of events throughout the year. From world-famous cultural festivals and sporting tournaments to historic parades and seasonal markets, there’s always something happening in the heart of the UK’s vibrant capital. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, these events showcase the spirit, creativity, and heritage of London, making every season special. Explore the highlights below and plan your next adventure in one of the world’s most dynamic cities.

London Fashion Week

When: February & September

A world-renowned event showcasing the latest trends in fashion, featuring top designers and emerging talent.

The Thames Festival

When: September

A celebration of London’s iconic river with arts, music, and food events along the banks of the Thames.

Notting Hill Carnival

When: August

Europe's largest street festival celebrating Caribbean culture with vibrant parades, music, and dancing.

London Film Festival

When: October

An international event celebrating cinema with screenings of the latest films from around the world.

New Year's Day Parade

When: January 1st

A lively parade featuring performers, musicians, and floats, kicking off the year with festive spirit.

Wimbledon

When: June-July

One of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments, held at the All England Club, attracting global attention.

The Lord Mayor’s Show

When: November

A historic parade celebrating the inauguration of the new Lord Mayor of London with floats, performances, and traditional ceremonies.

Hyde Park Winter Wonderland

When: November-January

A seasonal event featuring ice skating, a Christmas market, fairground rides, and a circus.

London Design Festival

When: September

A celebration of innovation in design, with installations, exhibitions, and talks throughout the city.

Pride in London

When: July

A massive LGBTQ+ celebration with a parade, performances, and events around the city.
