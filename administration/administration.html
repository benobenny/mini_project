Main administrative buildings

Links and description of the main administrative buildings in the City

Guildhall

Administrative center for the City of London Corporation. A historic building used for civic functions, events, and managing City affairs.

Mansion House

Official residence of the Lord Mayor of London. Hosts ceremonial events and mayoral functions.

City of London Police Headquarters

Law enforcement headquarters for the City of London Police. Responsible for maintaining law and order within the Square Mile.

London Fire Brigade - Dowgate Fire Station

Fire and rescue services. One of the key fire stations serving the City of London.

Bank of England

Central bank of the United Kingdom. Regulates monetary policy and is a cornerstone of the UK financial system.

St. Bartholomew's Hospital (Barts)

Emergency and general hospital services. A major healthcare institution in the City of London.

Old Bailey (Central Criminal Court)

England and Wales’ central criminal court. Handles high-profile criminal cases and is a significant judicial institution.

Tower Bridge Operational Centre

Manages operations for the iconic Tower Bridge. Ensures smooth functioning and maintenance of this vital transportation link.

City of London Coroner’s Court

Investigates unexplained or suspicious deaths within the City. Plays a vital role in the judicial process.

Barbican Estate Management Office

Administration of the Barbican residential and cultural complex. Oversees housing and cultural amenities in this prominent City area.

Bishopsgate Police Station

Police station within the City of London. Provides local law enforcement and community policing services.

City of London Magistrates’ Court

Handles minor criminal cases within the City. Operates under the judicial system of England and Wales.

St. Paul's Cathedral Chapter House

Administrative hub for St. Paul’s Cathedral. Manages religious and operational functions of the cathedral.

Port Health Authority Offices

Monitors food safety, water quality, and disease control in the Port of London. A critical administrative body ensuring public health standards.

City of London School for Boys Administration

Administrative offices for this prestigious school. Oversees educational and operational matters for one of the City’s key institutions.
